Operational Audit FAQ's


What’s a process?

A process is an event that triggers the execution of several tasks to achieve a desired outcome.

What’s a step?

A step can be defined as an action.

What’s a component?

A component is a constituent part of your product, for example, a component of a door (product) is the door knob (component).

If I have more components than the package I should be on, according to the products, what will I pay?

You will pay the package according to the number of components you need to get reviewed rather than the amount of products. As you can imagine, the complexity of the analysis scales up according to the number of components your product has.

How’s the payment done?

Our payment is system is in two trenches, 50% at beginning of the project and 50% at the end of it.If you’re outside the Eurozone, you can pay using a SWIFT transfer in Euros, otherwise if you’re in the Eurozone you can use a SEPA transfer.
