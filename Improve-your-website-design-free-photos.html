Author: Miguel Teixeira
May 2015
Tags: Resources, webdesign, photos

We came up with a list of the top 10 totally free photo sites that you can use to improve your website design.

1. What we mean by free

Are looking for free photos to use for a design and want to keep yourself out of copyright trouble? So you should to know this, there are the following types of free:

  • Creative Commons zero - you can use the photos in any way you'd like without asking permission.
  • Creative Commons with attribution -you can use the photo in any way you want but you need to attribute credits to the creator of the photo. Attribution is simple: When you include a photo in the web page, you need to identify the photographer ("Photo by John Doe") and include a link to the website.

All the websites presented are free of charge and the use of their pictures are attribution free (creative commons zero).
